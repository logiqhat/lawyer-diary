Request Data Deletion

You can ask us to delete your account data at any time. To start the process, send us an email and include the email address that is associated with your Lawyer Diary account.

Email us at support@lawyer-diary.com

To help us complete your request faster, please include:

  • Your account email (required).
  • Confirmation that you want your synced cases/dates removed from the cloud.
  • Any screenshots or notes that might help us locate your account if you use multiple emails (optional).

We will confirm deletion or ask for any clarification by email. If you only use the app offline, deleting the app from your device will remove local data; sending the request above removes any cloud copies.
